Blue Moon Gathering

By Ed Rogers on 2022-10-13

Come join the Madison Python community for an evening at Blue Moon Bar & Grill — 2535 University Ave. We'd like to get back in touch, discuss what we've all been up to, and plan future gatherings. We won't have a speaker or slides. Just friendly conversation. We welcome newcomers and experienced pythonistas alike.

Blue Moon has two floors inside — look for us upstairs. Blue Moon has a full menu of pub fare, including beverages (both with & without alcohol), burgers, sandwiches and sides.
